Of course the more fancier nights are the weekend nights, Friday, Saturday and Sunday nights. I have never been to Kora on a weekday night, you shouldn’t too. The clubs start to buzz from evening but the best part starts from 9 or 9:30 PM, that’s the best time to enter the club. The clubs end around 1 AM. Exit the clubs early if you want to catch a cab and avoid the rush.
